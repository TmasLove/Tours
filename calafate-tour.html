Itinerary Breakdown

Day 1 Activities
  • Airport Pickup
  • Free the rest of the day to visit the city
Day 2 Activities
  • Breakfast at the Hotel
  • Transfer to the Glaciers National Park at the agreed time of approximately 8:00 am
  • Full day Excursion begins
  • Los Glaciares National Park - Perito Moreno Glacier
  • This park has been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO.
  • The glacier, named after the great Argentinian explorer of the last century, is one of the few in the world.
  • The show is Indescribable. The waters slowly pierce a tunnel in the in front of the glacier until the pressure causes it to collapse. Waters rush in giant waves, and then start the whole process again
  • Return to Hotel around 18:00 hs
  • Free the rest of the day
Perito Moreno Glacier
Day 3 Activities
  • Breakfast at the Hotel
  • Transfer to the Port of Punta Bandera at the agreed time of approximately 7:30 am
  • Full day Excursion begins
  • Rivers of Ice Express (C/TRF - C/ENT)
  • 47 km from El Calafate, and consists of a navigation of approximately 5 hours visiting the most important glaciers of the National Park.
  • Enjoy the ride in one of the comfortable boats as we navigate and cross the Boca del Diablo and access the North Arm of Lake Argentino.
  • The Rios de Hielo excursion takes place during the high season in two hours: in the morning (Ríos de Hielo Express, daily frequency) and in the afternoon (Ríos de Hielo Sunset, day in between).
  • Important: Passangers will be informed of the boat departing times, safety guidelines and agreements.
  • Return to Hotel
  • Free the rest of the day
North Arm Lake of Argentina
