Day 1 Activities
  • Airport Pickup
  • Free the rest of the day to visit the city
Day 2 Activities
  • Breakfast at the Hotel
  • Transfer to the Iguazu National Park at the agreed time of approximately 7:30 am
  • Excursion begins
  • Garganta del Diablo circruit
  • Indescribable for it's beauty and energy that thrills everyone. Difficulty: without stairs
  • Superior Circuit
  • Access to the magnificent panoramic views of the falls and the delta of the Iguazú River formed by a set of leafy islands. Difficulty: without stairs
  • Lower Circruit
  • A set of strategically designed walkways that allow the most diverse views of the Falls and an intimate encounter with the waterfalls and charming corners of the jungle. Difficulty High: with stairs.
  • Return to Hotel around 4:00 pm
  • Free the rest of the day
Devils Throat Circuit

  • Breakfast at the Hotel

  • You will be welcomed by Natures warmth and exhibit the diverse colors of butterflys
  • Enjoy the Panaramic and 1,000 meter drop views! There is a viewpoint infront of Floriano accessed through an elevatpr which has the most impressive view!
  • Optional lunch, not included. For all excursions, We recommend carrying insect repellent, slippers and comfortable clothes.
  • Important: Consult the necessary documentation to cross the border
  • A unique opportunity to buy the best brands in the world without taxes, 10 minutes from the Falls. Open all year from 09:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.
