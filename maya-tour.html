Maya Tour

About Maya Tour


The Mayan civilization was among the original cultures of the New World and spanned more than 3,000 years.
The Mayans lived mainly on thea Yucatan Peninsula in the eastern one third of Mesoamerica and one of the most densely populated and culturally dynamic sociaties in the World.

In this guided tour, you will explore Mesoamerica through an 8 day guide. Check out the daily itineraries below.

Daily Itineraries

Day 1 Activities
  • Airport Pickup
  • Free the rest of the Day
Day 2 Activities
  • Transfer from your hotel to the Archaeological Zone of Chichén Itzá
  • Carry out the Be Maya program, in which you will have the experience of living certain activities carried out by the Mayans. Related to gastronomy and numerology
  • Guided tour of the Chichen Itza Archaeological Zone
  • Time to Eat
  • Return to Hotel
Chichen Itzá is famous worldwide for the play of light and shadow that at each equinox occurs on the stairway of the pyramidal basement known as El Castillo
Day 3 Activities
  • Hotel transfer to the archaeological zone of Uxmal
  • Mayan breakfast in the archaeological zone of Uxmal. Where you will have the opportunity to live traditions of a typical Yucatecan family
  • Guided tour of archaeological zone
  • Transfer to the Chocolate Museum "Choco Story" and guided tour of the site
  • Time to eat at the hotel restaurant The Lodge at Uxmal
  • Return to Hotel
Journey through time, you'll walk through a Sacbe, which comes from Mayan words "Sac" (white) and "be" (path). You will be able to know the different trees that our ancestors planted, until they reach a Mayan home, where they will learn how to make toritlllas, jacketed eggs, and you will be able to enjoy this delicious breakfast
Day 4 Activities
  • At your hotel you will receive the visit of an authentic Mayan priest, who will show you an original ritual practiced by our ancestors, the Mayans
  • Free the rest of the afternoonto to enjoy the Hacienda
Sacred rituals with Mayan priest
Day 5 Activities
  • Transfer from your hotel to the Hacienda Viva Sotuta de Peón
  • Guided private tour of the Hacienda Sotuta de Peón and time to enjoy its cenote
  • Lunch at the restaurant "Palapa" of the Hacienda Sotuta de Peón
  • Return to Hotel
Sotuta de Peon, Live Hacienda, is the restoration project of a landmark located in the heart of the ancient henequen zone in the Yucatan state, and gives a true glimpse of what was once a fully operational Henequen Hacienda in the grand style and tradition of this period.
Day 6 Activities
  • Transfer from your hotel to the Hacienda Casa D Aristi
  • Guided tour of the Casa D Aristi Hacienda, where you will be shown the process of making the Yucatecan drink Xtabentún
  • Transfer to the traditional Yucatecan, located in the historic center of the city, called "El Dzalbay", where you can enjoy snacks and regional drinks
  • Return to Hotel
Xtabentún is an ancient Mayan liqueur, very traditional and appreciated in the Yucatan Peninsula; It is made from fermented honey, work product of bees that pollinate the flower of the same name.
Day 7 Activities
  • Transfer from your hotel to the Yucatecan craft beer factory Patito
  • Guided tour of the Yucatecan craft brewery Patito, where you can learn about the process of making it
  • Time to eat at the Sister Republic Restaurant to taste the craft beer and enjoy a typical Yucatecan meal
  • Return to Hotel
Yucatecan brewery Patito
Day 8 Activities
  • Transfer from your hotel to the Mérida International Airport
